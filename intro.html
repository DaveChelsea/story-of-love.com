Introduction of Characters

Characters:-


•Armaan(AM) & Amaal(AML): I guess they are not needed to introduce😁. Never ever in life😆 coz everyone knows them. Well ARMAAN is the main character.


•Shravya(shravi aka S): A  sweet, kindhearted and cute girl. Who is loved by everyone and she loves her family the most. She loves to spend time with them & with nature(plants & animals)


•Anmol(An aka SB): Shravis cousin brother. Send to india for study & so that he can also learn Indian culture. A responsible & helping nature person. Comes back after years.


•Shravi's Dada(SDA): A lil bit strict for language & culture to family and also of joyful nature. Loves family but most lovely is shravi & anmol.


•Shravi's Dadi(SDI): Sweet in nature & always praying God for everyone good & peace in world.


•Shravi's Mom(Parvati aka P aka SM): A housewife after shravi's birth, not completely🙄 coz she sometimes goes to her company when it is needed.


•Shravi's Father(Aalok aka Ak aka SF): A businessman & also manages her wife company too.


•Shravi's Aunt(Aarti aka Ai aka SA): Her aunt takeover the family business after her father as shravi's father wants to do his own business. She loves shravi as her child, she is her father's small sister(foi).


•Shravi's Uncle(Shrirang aka S aka SU): C.E.O in her wife's company. Take care of everyone in family. Anmol is the dearest to him & shravi too.


•Shravi's friend (Aadhya aka Aa aka SF): Sweet & simple like shravi. Cares for shravi too much.


•Raghu(Driver aka D): A kindhearted & faithful person. Everyone behaves like a family member with him.
